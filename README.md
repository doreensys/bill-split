# Smart Bill Splitter

Solves the "too many cooks" problem for group bills. Instead of one
person reading the receipt and doing math by hand, everyone joins a
room, picks their own items, and the app figures out who owes who — in
the fewest payments possible.

## How it works

1. Host creates a room, adds items from the receipt, gets a room code.
2. Friends join and tap the items they had (shared items get tapped by
   multiple people).
3. Anyone who fronted money (put the bill on their card) logs how much.
4. App computes shares and settles the group up.
